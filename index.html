Cloud computing is a form of distributed computing that provides its users the ability to use a wide range of resources in computer networks to complete their work. The resources are scaled dynamically and are provided as a service over the Internet . Users do not need knowledge, experience or control over the technology infrastructure. By using cloud computing companies become greener because they decrease their energy consumption to increase capacity without investing in more infrastructure. It also increases hardware utilization rate since resources are shared.
